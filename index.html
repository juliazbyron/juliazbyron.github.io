About me

Coming from a background in industrial design, I've switched gears into the tech industry. Wherever I may find myself, what persists is a passion for using a holistic, empathic approach to addressing real world problems through user-centric research and design. At my current position at IBM, I am pushing to establish the practice of design and user research from the ground up in an engineering-driven culture. I've established user research on my team by setting up a user feedback program, research processes and education for the greater team. Now that that is off the ground and running, I am designing in addition to researching on our mobile backend services platform. I love the challenge of advocating for these efforts and ensuring our users' needs manifest in the final product.
